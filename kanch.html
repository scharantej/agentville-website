Blue Diamond Growers, the world's leading almond marketer and processor, today announced the opening of its new Almond Innovation Center in Sacramento, California. New Study Finds Almonds May Help Reduce Risk of Heart Disease

A new study published in the Journal of the American Heart Association found that eating almonds may help reduce the risk of heart disease. The study, which was conducted by researchers at the University of California, Davis, found that people who ate almonds had lower levels of LDL (bad) cholesterol and higher levels of HDL (good) cholesterol.

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has announced a recall of almonds due to potential Salmonella contamination. The recall affects almonds sold by the following companies: Blue Diamond, Mariani Nut Company, and Paramount Farms. The almonds were distributed to retailers nationwide.
