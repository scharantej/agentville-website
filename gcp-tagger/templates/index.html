GCP Project Tagging App

This app allows you to tag your GCP projects with Salesforce opportunity IDs to prevent their deletion.

  • To add a tag, enter the project ID and Salesforce opportunity ID in the form below and click "Add Tag".
  • To remove a tag, click the "X" next to the tag.
