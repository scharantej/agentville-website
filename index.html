Autonomous Agents

Autonomous agents are software programs that can operate without human intervention. They are able to make decisions and take actions on their own, based on their programming and the data they collect.

Autonomous agents are used in a variety of applications, such as:

  • Self-driving cars
  • Robotics
  • Medical diagnosis
  • Financial trading

The potential applications of autonomous agents are vast. They have the potential to revolutionize many industries and improve our lives in a number of ways.

However, there are also a number of challenges associated with autonomous agents. For example, they can be difficult to develop and maintain, and they can be vulnerable to security breaches
